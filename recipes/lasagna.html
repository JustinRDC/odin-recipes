Description

Lasagna is a layered baked pasta dish of Italian origin, typically featuring wide, flat pasta sheets alternating with various fillings, such as ragù (meat sauce), béchamel sauce, and cheeses like ricotta and mozzarella

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

    7. Layering the lasagna

  7. Meat sauce
  8. Noodles
  9. Ricotta mixture
  10. Mozzarella slices
  11. Meat sauce
  12. Parmesan cheese
  13. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
